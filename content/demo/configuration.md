Image Compressor ships with sensible defaults, but advanced users can fine-tune output quality, rename patterns, overwrite behavior, and destination handling from the preferences panel. Each option updates instantly and applies to batch exports.

图片压缩器默认配置合理，高级用户可以在偏好设置面板中微调输出质量、重命名模式、覆盖行为和目标处理。每个选项都会即时更新并应用于批量导出。
